About TFCG

A House of Prayer, Purpose & Power

The Faith Centre Global is a place where ordinary people develop extraordinary faith. We are a multicultural, non-denominational, Word of Faith, Prophetic and Apostolic based church led by Pastor Godspower Opara Martins — a community where everyone can belong, believe, and be more.

Learn More About Us

Our Vision

We exist to Re-Present God to the lost and found for transformation in Christ — multi-generational, multi-ethnic, multi-plying, and multi-campus.

Our Mandate

To REACH, WIN, TEACH & TRAIN disciples who will serve their generation.
