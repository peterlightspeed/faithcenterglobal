About The Faith Centre Global

Our story, our beliefs, and our leadership

Our Story

A Foundation of Faith

The Faith Centre Global is a place where ordinary people develop extraordinary faith, and become the powerful leaders God created them to be. Discover your place and your destiny in God's Kingdom, as you explore your future at The Faith Centre Global.

The Faith Centre Global is a WORD OF FAITH, PROPHETIC AND APOSTOLIC based church led by Pastor Godspower Opara Martins. We are a multicultural and non-denominational church of real people seeking a meaningful life and real relationship with God. Community is our passion, and we strive to live that mission by making an impact in our local community and beyond — building a place where everyone can belong, believe, and be more.

Whether you have a Pentecostal or Baptist background, or no church background at all, The Faith Centre Global is where you can belong.

Our Vision

We exist to Re-Present God to the lost and found for transformation in Christ. We are a multi-church — meaning we are multi-generational, multi-ethnic, multi-plying, and multi-campus.

Our Mandate

The Faith Centre Global is committed to demonstrating God's unconditional love to the entire world. Our mandate is to REACH, WIN, TEACH & TRAIN disciples who will serve their generation.

Core Beliefs

Statement of Faith

1

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

2

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

3

We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, and His miracles.

4

We believe in His vicarious and atoning death through His shed blood, His bodily resurrection, and ascension.

5

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a godly life.

6

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues.

7

We believe in divine healing, the prophetic ministry, and the apostolic mandate of the church.

8

We believe in the spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ.

Leadership

Meet Our Lead Pastor

Pastor Godspower Opara Martins

Lead Pastor, TFCG

Pastor Godspower is an anointed teacher of the Word, operating heavily in the prophetic and apostolic offices. With a divine mandate to liberate men from the shackles of darkness, his ministry is characterized by deep scriptural insights, accurate prophetic declarations, and notable miracles.

He is passionate about raising a congregation of believers who not only know the Word but demonstrate its power in their everyday lives.
